HISTORY OF ZAMBOANGA CITY

Discover the fascinating past of Zamboanga City!



Our History!

Zamboanga City is called the "Sardines Capital of the Philippines".

This is because 9 of the 12 sardine companies in the country are in the city.

Other names for the city are "Asia's Latin City" and "City of Flowers".

The city of Zamboanga is politically divided into 98 barangays or barrios.

Zamboanga was chosen in 1569 as the site of the Spanish settlement

and garrison on La Caldera (now part of Barangay Recodo). Spain

granted the former Rajahnate of Sanmalan protectorate status against

the Sulu Sultanat, its former overlord and the kingdom's name was hispanized

into Zamboanga and made a city.
